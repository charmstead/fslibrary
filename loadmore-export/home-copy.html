Partners

_War Child UK

background color

War Child UK works to protect, educate, and stand up for the rights of children living in conflict by providing safe spaces, education opportunities, and psychological support.

_Physicians for Human Rights

background color

Physicians for Human Rights uses science and the uniquely credible voices of medical professionals to document and call attention to severe human rights violations around the world.

_Human Rights Law Centre

background color

Human Rights Law Centre uses strategic legal action, policy solutions, and advocacy to eliminate inequality and injustice and build a fairer, more compassionate society.

_GOAL

background color

GOAL delivers a wide range of humanitarian and development programs, with a focus on systems, partnerships, and building resilience for the world’s poorest and most vulnerable communities.

_Allies Against Slavery

background color

Allies Against Slavery harnesses the power of technology, data, and partnerships to combat human trafficking and protect freedom and dignity.

_Larkin Street Youth Services

background color

Larkin Street Youth Services offers a comprehensive spectrum of services to young people (ages 12-24) experiencing homelessness including housing, education, employment, and wellness programs so youth can move from crisis to stability.

_WITNESS

background color

WITNESS harnesses the power of video and technology to help global activists document human rights abuses and distribute their stories in safe and ethical ways that lead to lasting change.
